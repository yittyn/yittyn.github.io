### Optimizing the Blog Workflow

To keep shipping daily without getting bogged down by repetitive manual setups, I optimized the AI-assisted writing pipeline for this blog today.

Instead of re-explaining tone and formatting guidelines for every post, I set up a structured system using the IDE's customization schema:
- **Project Rules (`AGENTS.md`):** Establishes permanent project context, voice, and vocabulary constraints.
- **Skill Playbook (`SKILL.md`):** Automates procedural guidelines for converting raw drafts into final published posts.
