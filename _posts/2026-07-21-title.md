### Streamlining the AI Agent Workflow

To make this blog-logging process as fast and repeatable as possible, I spent some time configuring my development setup. 

I split the system configuration into two clean layers:
* **Workspace Rules (`.agents/AGENTS.md`):** Defines global constraints—my persona, tone, style rules, and vocabulary choices so the AI never adds unwanted filler or alters my voice.
* **Skill Playbooks (`.agents/skills/SKILL.md`):** Procedural step-by-step guides that trigger on specific tasks (like taking raw notes from `draft/` and outputting clean markdown to `publish/`).

Separating rules (what to do) from skills (how to do it) keeps the agent workflow modular, reliable, and ridiculously fast to iterate on.
